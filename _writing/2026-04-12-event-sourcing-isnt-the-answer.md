Every few years, event sourcing makes the rounds again. The pitch is
seductive: a perfect audit trail, time travel, no more "why is this row
in this state?" mysteries. The reality, in most teams I've worked with,
is that you trade one class of problem for three new ones.

This post is the long version of an argument I keep having in design
reviews.
